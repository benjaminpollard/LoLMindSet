LoL MindSet

LoL MindSet is an Android app(iOS Soon to Follow) that allows users to get a posative insight in to the recent proformance of themselves and others in there current game match. To help inform gameplay desions, such as lanes to gank for or to roam to. Or how well your lane oppenent has faired in their latest games. Currentlly in Beta

Features:
-Insight in to winning and losing streaks
-Showing if a player is playing Normally, Well or Great
-Suppors Ranked Games on Summoners Rift(Oher Games Modes coming soon)
