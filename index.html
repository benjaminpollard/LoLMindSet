LoL MindSet - BETA

LoL MindSet is an Android app(iOS Soon to Follow) that allows users to get a positive insight in to the recent preformance of themselves and others in there current game match. To help inform gameplay decisions, such as lanes to gank for or roam to and how well your lane oppenent has faired in their latest games. Currentlly in Beta

Features:
-Insight in to winning and losing streaks
-Showing if a player is playing Normally, Well or Great
-Supports Ranked Games on Summoners Rift(Other Games Modes coming soon)



Download APK
How to Use: At first you will presented with a screen to enter your summoner name and Region. Thats checked to see if the details are correct. Then it brings you to a screen, which when you are in a game will search your team mates and give you insight in to the mind set of everyone in the game. With a buton to re check once you get in to a new game. If you want to change the account your viewing then tap the setings cog on the stats page, then press the Enter Account Details Button.
